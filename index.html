Rules

Team for Individuals
Individual people who doesn’t have a team will be assigned a team. Team matching will be optimized so that a team doesn’t end up with either all low-rank player or all high-rank players.
Tournaments
Competitive : If the teams are less the Tournament will be of Round-Robin nature. Every team will play every team. For every :
WIN : 2 scores
LOSE: 0 scores
DRAW: 1 score each
Otherwise it’ll be an elimination tournament.
DeathMatch : There will also be 10 people deathmatch. Which is an individual programme.

About Championship

This championship is organized by two Individuals who share a keen interest for gaming. Especially VALORANT.

We are not any professionals, and we are organizing this gaming tournament for the very first time, so if we do any mistakes, bear with us. Also a sincere apology in advance for any mistakes.

If you have any suggestions or questions regarding the championship, feel free to submit them.
